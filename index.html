An Update

ProjectCSGIRLS
As a lead member of the ProjectCSGIRLS Outreach Team, I've been consistently contacting and maintaining connections with many schools and organizations, publicizing and informing students of the opportunity to conduct their own research in the computer science and technology fields and develop a new contraption tackling a social issue that would be ultimately entered in the annual competition we hold. At the beginning of this month, registration closed and I am now proud to say that over 500 girls have been drawn to the organization, a significant increase in number from the previous year.
